Continuing Education Information

The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education as a provider of continuing pharmacy education.

This activity provides 2 hours (0.2 CEUs) of continuing pharmacy education credit (ACPE activity #204-000-10-475-L05P).

Attendees must complete a Continuing Pharmacy Education Request online and may immediately print their official statements of continuing pharmacy education credit at the ASHP Learning Center at http://ce.ashp.org following the activity. For complete activity information, visit www.ashpadvantage.com/btupdate.
